About Me

Arun SP is the Director of Data Governance & Management at iLink Digital — a global technology services organization. As one of the youngest directors in the company, he leads the Data Governance practice overseeing strategy, pre-sales, solution engineering, partnerships, and client engagements — working directly with CIOs, CTOs, and CDOs to shape data and AI strategies that are executable, not just aspirational.

With over seven years of experience at Cognizant, EY, and Tiger Analytics, Arun has built a reputation for combining deep technical expertise with strong business leadership. He is passionate about helping organizations build data foundations that enable analytics, AI, and better decision-making.

He is among the few professionals who actively contribute across sales, pre-sales, partnerships & alliances, and delivery — bridging the gap between business strategy and technical execution.

Arun began his career in Civil Engineering before pivoting into data and analytics — a testament to his belief that career growth is defined not by where one starts, but by the willingness to learn, take ownership, and create meaningful impact.
